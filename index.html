❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Prayer times seem incorrect. What can I do?

Go to Settings → Calculation Method and choose the method used in your region (e.g., Diyanet for Turkey, ISNA for North America, MWL for Europe).

I'm not receiving prayer notifications.

Make sure notifications are enabled in both the app settings and your device's Settings → Notifications → Nûr Vakti.

The Qibla compass is not accurate.

Calibrate your device by moving it in a figure-8 motion. Also ensure no magnets or metal objects are nearby.

Can I use the app offline?

Prayer times and most features work offline after the initial setup. Quran audio streaming and Islamic AI require an internet connection.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings → Language and choose between Turkish and English.
